## EMOTIONAL ARC
This episode is about the clash between external pressure and internal stillness. The Trainer's chaotic, well-meaning energy is an absurd assault on Walter's quiet world. The viewer is left with a feeling of deadpan comedy, but also the warmth that comes from someone, anyone, showing up and refusing to let Walter remain forgotten, even if their methods are ridiculous.

## STORY ARC (honest assessment)
This episode follows a **Sustained Contrast** or **Immovable Object** structure. It is not a traditional narrative arc.
*   **Setup:** Establishes a baseline of extreme quiet and stillness (Walter's world).
*   **Inciting Incident:** A sudden, violent intrusion of extreme noise and energy (The Trainer's arrival).
*   **Escalation:** The intrusion's energy intensifies its attempts to affect the baseline (The Trainer's shouting and gesturing).
*   **Climax/Resolution:** The baseline remains completely unaffected. The story ends not with a change, but by holding on the unresolved, absurd tableau of the immovable object (Walter) and the unstoppable force (The Trainer's music/energy) coexisting. The punchline is the lack of resolution.
