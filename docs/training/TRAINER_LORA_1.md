*   **Inciting Incident/Disruption:** The Trainer arrives, shattering the quiet with loud music and manic energy.
*   **Escalation:** The Trainer's attempts to motivate Walter escalate from a direct command to a more desperate, high-energy barrage.
*   **Climax/Sustained Punchline:** Walter's absolute non-reaction is held against the Trainer's peak energy, creating a sustained comedic tableau. The episode ends not with a resolution, but by holding on this unresolved, absurd new status quo.
